Graphic Design Course

Course Overview

Master the art of visual communication. This comprehensive course covers Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, typography, and branding.

What You'll Learn

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • InDesign
  • Typography
  • Branding

Course Content

Get introduced to the fundamentals of graphic design, including design principles, color theory, and the history of design.

Learn the basics of Adobe Photoshop, including tools, techniques, and workflows for photo editing and graphic creation.

Explore Adobe Illustrator techniques for vector graphics, including creating illustrations, logos, and complex shapes.

Learn Adobe InDesign for creating print materials such as brochures, flyers, and magazines with advanced layout techniques.

Understand the principles of typography, including font selection, layout, and the impact of type on visual communication.

Learn how to develop a cohesive brand identity, including logo design, brand guidelines, and visual consistency.

Instructors

This course is taught by experienced graphic designers with years of industry experience.
